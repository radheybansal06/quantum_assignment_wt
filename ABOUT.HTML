Who We Are

The Quantum Club is a student-led initiative at C.V. Raman Global University that fosters curiosity, innovation, and collaboration in quantum computing and related technologies.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge and skills in emerging technologies, preparing them for the future.

Our Vision

To become a hub of innovation, learning, and collaboration in quantum technology research and development.

Our Team

A passionate group of students and faculty advisors dedicated to creating meaningful opportunities.
